How to Perfectly Boil an Egg

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs

Directions

Step 1

Place eggs in a sauce pan and pour in cold water to cover; place over high heat. When water starts to simmer, turn off heat, cover the pan with a lid, and let stand for 17 minutes. Don't peek.

Step 2

Pour out hot water and pour cold water over eggs. Drain and refill with cold water; let stand until eggs are cool, about 20 minutes.

Step 3

Peel eggs under running water.
